Travel Requirements For Mumbai

A card ticket has a date and time stamp on it. Your journey must begin within one hour of the time stamped on the ticket. For a return journey, the ticket is valid till midnight of the following day. If the return ticket is purchased on a Saturday, it will be valid till Monday at midnight.
